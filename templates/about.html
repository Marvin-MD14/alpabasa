THE KAAGAPAY PROGRAM

ABOUT

Kaagapay is a program designed to support students in developing their reading and comprehension skills. It aims to strengthen literacy, provide proper guidance, and encourage a love for reading as a foundation for lifelong learning.

The Challenge

In today’s rapidly changing world of information, it is essential for every child to develop strong reading skills. Kaagapay aims to address the challenges faced by students, particularly difficulties in reading comprehension.

A Mission for the Future

Overall, Kaagapay is not just an educational program; it is a mission that highlights the importance of reading as a key to knowledge, opportunities, and a better future.
